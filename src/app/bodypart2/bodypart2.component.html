What is The Dime?

The DIME functions like a mutual fund. DIMEs can be purchased in Bitcoin or Ether at the Net Asset Value of the fund (Initial Offering Price of $10 USD) on a monthly basis. The purchaser will receive an ERC20 Standard Token (a DIME) and their ownership of that Token will be maintained on the Ethereum blockchain. The proceeds are then allocated in proportions that aim to track the performance of The DIME Index, a market cap weighted index of the top ten cryptocurrencies, managed by Stack Financial.
