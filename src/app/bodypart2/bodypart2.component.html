What is The DIME?

The DIME functions similarly to a mutual fund with additional transparency provided by the blockchain. DIMEs can be purchased in Bitcoin or Ether at the Net Asset Value of the fund (Initial Offering Price of $10 USD) on a monthly basis. The purchaser will receive an ERC20 Standard Token (a DIME) and their ownership of that Token will be maintained on the Ethereum blockchain. The proceeds are allocated in proportions that aim to track the performance of The DIME Index, a market cap weighted index of the ten largest cryptocurrencies.

Investors can opt to redeem their DIMEs in exchange for the NAV in Bitcoin or Ether at anytime. New purchases and redemptions both occur at the monthly rebalancing.
