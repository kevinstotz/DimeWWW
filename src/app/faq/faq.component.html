FAQ


During each Order Period, we will be accept Ether and Bitcoin for purchases of DIMEs. You may send any coin as many times as you want, and in any combination. All transactions are recorded and displayed clearly on the account page.


How do I buy Bitcoin or Ether?


The most convenient and cheapest way to buy Bitcoin or Ether is through Coinbase. Or steps:
  1. Open a Coinbase account
  2. Add your payment method (Credit card or bank account)
  3. Go to “Buy/Sell” and select the amount of Ethereum you desire
  4. Click “Buy Bitcoin” or “Buy Ethereum”

Can I invest with cash or credit card?


Yes, we will accept contributions in the form of Visa or MasterCard.


What is the minimum I can invest?


The minimum price of an Order Period is $50.


What are the Fees?


The Purchase of a fund has. First, a 2% per annum Management Fee. Second, a 20% fee of profits for each rebalance period. These fees are some of the lowest in the industry and are used to operate the business.


If I want to sell, how do I do that?


The products are redeemed and not sold. They cannot be sold in the secondary market. Instead, the product can be redeemed to Yogi's House at their Net Asset Value on a rebalancing date. At anytime, holders can place an order to redeem their position and on the following rebalancing date, that position will be redeemed. The steps can be broken into:
  1. Login to your account on Yogi's House
  2. Follow the steps to Withdraw
  3. Go to “Withdraw” and select the amount you desire to withdraw
  4. Select the ETH/ERC20 Wallet where you wish to receive your Ether
