How does The DIME work? The DIME functions much like a mutual fund offered on an interval basis. DIMEs can be purchased in Bitcoin or Ether (Initial Offering Price of $10 USD) on a monthly basis. A purchaser will receive an ERC20 Standard Token and their ownership of that Token will be maintained on the Ethereum blockchain. The Bitcoin or Ether used to purchase the DIME is pooled with Bitcoin and Ether from other purchasers and used to acquire a basket of cryptocurrencies in proportion to The DIME Index. When a DIMEholder is ready sell, they can redeem their DIME for the net asset value in Bitcoin or Ether. Redeem Anytime

Holders can redeem their DIMEs are anytime and will receive their funds on the subsequent rebalancing date.
