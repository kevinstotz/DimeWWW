How does The DIME work?

The DIME functions much like a mutual fund. DIMEs can be purchased in Ether (Initial Offering Price of $10 USD) on a quarterly basis. The purchaser will receive an ERC20 Standard Token and their ownership of that Token will be maintained on the Ethereum blockchain. That Ether is pooled with the Ether of other purchasers and used to acquire a basket of cryptocurrencies in proportion to The DIME Index, a market cap weighted index of the top ten cryptocurrencies.
