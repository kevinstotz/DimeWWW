Keep It Simple

Investing in Bitcoin is complicated. Investing in a diverse and secure portfolio of ten digital assets is extremely complicated and by all accounts not feasible for the average investor. The DIME solves this problem, allowing users to hold a secure and diverse portfolio of cryptocurrencies through the possession of a single ERC-20 Token.
